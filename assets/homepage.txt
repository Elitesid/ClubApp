Rotaract Club of Smart City Navi Mumbai is a Youth wing of Rotary
Club of Smart City Navi Mumbai and has been operational for the
past five years

We have a manner strength of 60 with Youth ranging from
working professionals to Graduate students

Rotaract club of Smart City is a club registered in Rotary
International District 3142 which has its operations in Navi
Mumbai, Thane and beyond

Rotaract focuses on the development of young adults as
leaders and is a platform for young men and women to
enhance the knowledge and skills that will assist them in
personal development, to address the physical and social needs
of their communities
